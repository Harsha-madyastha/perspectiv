Here's about me

My journey commenced with the belief that 'Life is a race … If you don’t run fast… you will be like a broken andaa…'. My perspective on life took a significant turn in 2014 when I relocated to Bangalore for my Bachelor's. It was during this pivotal period that I purchased my very first camera, forever altering the way I perceived the world.

I became enamored with capturing moments that intrigued me, and my photographic journey began with a keen focus on birds, a passion that still holds a special place in my heart, although my current circumstances don't permit me to pursue it as ardently.

While traversing theWestern Ghats, I discovered that nature conceals its own secrets, tucked away in its hidden pockets. Often, I find myself yearning to pause the unfolding scene, only to hit 'play' and immerse myself in its beauty.

Bangalore provided an ideal launchpad for my explorations, and later, I embarked on a new chapter in Germany. Initially, the people, architecture, cuisine, streets, and various other aspects of this foreign land gradually became intertwined with my perspective. It was during this time that I delved into traveling to European cities whenever I could spare a moment, simultaneously earning a livelihood.

My extensive travels have brought me to a profound realization: every city possesses a unique voice, and it's up to us to lend an attentive ear. Life is an intricate tapestry of untold stories, and I'm here to listen, capturing the whispers of the world's diverse and captivating cities.
